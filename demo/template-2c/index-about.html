Chris Hunt is a fashion and advertising photographer based in New York City.

Beginning his career as a photojournalist, Chris then moved into fashion and advertising photography after working as a model agent for several years in Los Angeles. He has recently broadened his portfolio into film, directing TV commercials for fashion and lifestyle brands. Originally from California, he has spent the last 15 years working all around the world.
